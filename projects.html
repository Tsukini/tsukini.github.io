FUMIKO

About

Fumiko was originally a project for our course of Computer Science on University. In the beginning, it was to finish there but, as we made it, we it saw that was a good project (for that moment was) and we wanted to study about game devolopment and stuffs so, we decided to continue it.

The game suffered a lot of changes. The first idea was to be a platformer game, the scenario was to be our University and the objective was to defeat our professors. As we had a tiny time to do it and we had to search and study about the engine, we decided to change it to this.

There is no history on the game, you just have to go down, collect the coins and kill the ants! (Why ants? I have no idea! haha)

The project project is available on GitHub. We appreciate any help.
